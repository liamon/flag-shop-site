About Us

Based in Galway, Ireland, Get Flagged! is the largest online store devoted specifically to buying flags in the entire world. Founded in 2006, we have steadily grown from our small beginnings to the position we are in now. We ship to over 110 territories. Instead of being bought from outside companies, all of our flags are manufactured by us in-house, so quality is guaranteed. We also produce and sell historical and regional flags.
